About

I am a third-year undergraduate in Artificial Intelligence at the Macau University of Science and Technology. I am broadly interested in large language models and AI for software engineering—and, more generally, in how to make machine learning reliable when it meets messy, real-world problems.

That question runs through my work. I led a first-authored empirical study on uncertainty quantification for software defect prediction (under review at ACM TOSEM), and during a research assistantship at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (under Prof. Shaoyuan Li) I applied machine learning to industrial- and chemical-process modeling. I enjoy working across domains—turning a real problem into a method, and a method into something that actually runs.
