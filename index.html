About

I am a third-year undergraduate in Artificial Intelligence at the Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST). My research interests center on large language models and AI for software engineering, and I have broad hands-on experience applying machine learning to industrial- and chemical-process modeling. In my sophomore year I worked as a research assistant at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (Department of Automation), under the guidance of Prof. Shaoyuan Li. I have (co-)authored four papers, including a first-authored empirical study currently under review at ACM TOSEM.
